About Time Zone Buddies

About Time Zone Buddies Time Zone Buddies was born from the chaos of mismatched clocks and missed messages—now it’s your go-to tool for staying in sync across the globe. Whether it’s morning in Mumbai or midnight in Manhattan, this playful, animated app helps you coordinate with friends, teammates, or collaborators in different time zones with ease. Designed with glowing buttons, smooth invite boxes, and a dash of creative flair, it’s more than just a scheduler—it’s a celebration of global connection. Crafted by a curious coder with a love for both function and fun, Time Zone Buddies turns time differences into opportunities for smoother chats, better planning, and stronger bonds.

Built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, it features glowing buttons, animated invite boxes, and a sleek UI — all part of my journey into creative web development.
